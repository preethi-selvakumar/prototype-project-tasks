Makeup

Basic Makeup

Basic makeup typically refers to a simple and natural makeup look that enhances your features without being too heavy or dramatic.

HD Makeup

HD makeup, also known as High Definition makeup, is a makeup technique and product range specifically designed to create a flawless and camera-ready look.

4K Makeup

Makeup artists who work in the television or film industry, particularly those dealing with 4K cameras and ultra-high-definition filming, may need to adapt their makeup techniques and products to ensure that the makeup looks flawless on-screen and withstands the scrutiny of the high-resolution format. However, the principles of achieving a smooth and natural look, as well as using high-quality, camera-ready makeup products, are similar to those of HD makeup.

Transfer Proof Makeup

Transfer-proof or waterproof makeup is designed to stay in place and resist smudging, smearing, or transferring onto other surfaces, even when exposed to water, sweat, or humidity. This type of makeup is especially useful in situations where you want your makeup to last for an extended period without the need for touch-ups. Here are some key transfer-proof makeup products.

Airbrush Makeup

Airbrush makeup is a technique of applying makeup using an airbrush system, which sprays a fine mist of makeup onto the skin. It's a silicon based makeup The airbrush makeup technique provides a flawless, lightweight, and long-lasting finish.
