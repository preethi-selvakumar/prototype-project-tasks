FAQs?

Is there a speed limit?

Zara cars allows up to 125 km/hr. However it is 80 km/hr in a few cities where some cars might be equipped with speed governors as per government directives. Zara cars strictly advises to follow local speed limits.

Can I extend/ cancel/ modify?

Yes, extensions are possible subject to availability & charges. Cancellations & modifications will attract nominal charges as per our policy.

Booking criteria & documents?

Min. 21 years old, have a valid original government ID (Aadhar or Passport) and an original hard copy or a DigiLocker of a driving license for “Light Motor Vehicles,” which is at least 6 months old at the time of starting the trip. We may ask for additional documents for verification in some cases, e.g., local ID or proof of travel

Are there any restricted areas?

Leh/Ladhakh, Spiti Valley & Kaza/Nako regions are not permitted to take Zara cars. Customer will be fully liable for any damages incurred to the car in that region
